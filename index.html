I am a first year computational and applied mathematics PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin Oden institute. My research interests are concentrated in theoretical and mathematical neuroscience. In particular, I am interested in the development of quantitative theory concerning both the cognitive and neural scales of learning.

Before starting my PhD, I spent two years as a postbacc at the National Insitute of Mental Health in Angela Langdon's Unit on the Neural Computations in Learning. I did my bachelor's in pure math and biochemistry at the University of Florida.
